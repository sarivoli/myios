sMatcher is a tool which will help you to find the "Star Porutham".
"Star Porutham" is a important process in South Indian marriages.

It will be the first step in the Indian marriages before even look at the profile.
Normally this "Star Porutham" will be calculated based on couples Birth Star and Rasi.

sMatcher will be giving you the result based on general algorithm used for star match.
Please contact your Astrologer for the final result, we are not responsible for any issues caused by this tool. Please let us know if you have any suggestions or comments.
